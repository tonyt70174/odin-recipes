Lasagna

Description

This lasagna dish features a robust tomato sauce, fresh herbs, and four cheeses.

This dish could be enjoyed by everyone!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Cook Meat
  3. Assemble Lasagna
  4. Put in Oven for 45 mins at 375 degrees
