Pan Seared Steak

Description

This Pan-Seared Steak has a garlic rosemary-infused butter that makes it taste steakhouse quality.

You'll be impressed by how easy it is to make!

Ingredients

  • New York Strip Steaks or your steak of choice
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic
  • Fresh rosemary

Steps

  1. Pat dry the steaks
  2. Heat the pan and add oil
  3. Drop in steaks for sear
  4. Turn on all sides
  5. Reduce heat and add butter, garlic, rosemary
  6. Spoon butter over steak until done
  7. Transfer to cutting board, cover, and let it rest for 10 mins
  8. Slice it up and enjoy
